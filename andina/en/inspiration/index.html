Inspiration

Overview, Insights & Look Book

Every idea is born from a moment of inspiration, and with this platform we seek to convey this feeling to those who were part of the development of our collections and reflect our inspiration in all those designers who decide to get involved in the adventure of working with our yarns.
Inspiration
Our inspiration for the AW 24-25 Yarn Collection

The encounter between the Andean cosmovision and haute couture fashion converge in a mixture of sensations that derives in the main inspiration for our AW 24-25 collection, which presents the best of both worlds: the ancient tradition of the Andes and the style trendy avant-garde.

Inspiration in colors
The base colours for our collection

Nature is a work of art itself. From the intense colour of a sunset, to the softness of a rose; we have sought to reflect the natural wonders in our color palette of the new collection, which breathes trends in each of its finishes.,

The Alpaca
Treasure of the Andes
Warmth, resistance, softness and high fineness are the main characteristics of the fur of our Peruvian alpaca, which is presented in this collection as the central axis of a series of yarns that take advantage of its purity, as well as highlighting its qualities when they are part of Premium mixes with other materials of unmatched quality, such as cashmere, yak, fine merinos, silk, linen pima cotton, among others.
Our lines
For Machine and Hand Knitting
Hand knitting
Terra Line
There are styles that do not expire despite the passing of the years and the Terra line for hand knitting is precisely that alternative that fits into traditional projects, with its classic round yarns based on alpaca, merino wool and other natural fibres.
Terra is manifested through classic yarns in its shape but varied in terms of its color chart and the innovative effects that are proposed for this new season, which pose a unique combination between the traditional and the upcoming trends.

Cloud Line
Our new Cloud line symbolizes the union of alpaca and a range of outstanding natural fibres in fancy yarns effects, which promises to stimulate your imagination and be the support of projects that take you to another level.
Dare to experiment with the characteristics of these yarns, which are light, bulky and with unique textures that allow you to create innovative garments or accessories.

Eco line
This exclusive line presents 100% natural qualities, as well as our latest innovation in colours "Bothanical Dyes" that are dyed exclusively with plants, you can find it in our Eco line for hand knitting.
Here you will find mainly fancy yarns made of natural fibres that are complemented by soft and earth tones that come from the natural colours of alpaca, peruvian wool and flora from our Andean mountains, which have a subtle delicacy and are the most environmentally friendly.

Pure Line
This line was born because the celebration of 90th anniversary at Michell, where we wanted to pay tribute to the finest qualities which we work with. This year we present a second version, always with exclusive fibers and a refined look.
Pure line presents bespoke yarns both classic and fancy made in 100% Super Baby Alpaca, 100% Baby Suri, 100% Baby Llama, 100% Mulberry Silk, 100% Cashmere and 100% Extra Fine Wool.,

Machine Knitting
Terra Line
The concept of the Terra line for machine knitting is to use the best natural fibres, such as alpaca, wool, silk, cashmere and cotton; with the aim of giving shape to modern and attractive pieces, both in knitting and weaving fabrics, which stand out for their finesse and their innovative colour chart that is on trend and projection for the new season.,

Cloud Line
The Cloud line has been elaborated for designing garments that are light and that are at the forefront of fashion.
The idea of Cloud is based on offering a blend of the best natural fibres and some synthetic fibers, in yarns suitable for all types of garments and fabrics that require softness and fineness.,

Eco line
The Eco line allows us to generate a direct and unique connection with nature. The softness and delicacy of the range of colours that alpaca offers us, whose beautiful natural tones are combined with other high-quality fibres such as Peruvian pima cotton and silk, are the basis of this luxurious collection, which is 100% undyed.
Conocer más
Prestige Line
The finest fibres in the world converge in a line that projects elegance in each of its yarns. Prestige stands out for the luxury and precision in the details when dealing with the unique softness of fibres such as Superbaby Alpaca, Cashmere, Extra Fine Merino Wool and Silk in exclusive blends with materials that are characterized by their delicacy.
These bespoke yarns have been developed for flat knitting. The collection is aimed at an exclusive audience that knows how to recognise and appreciate the unique sensations offered by the most exquisite materials in the textile market.,
Conocer más
Sock line
The Sock line proposes interesting alternatives for the creation socks for all occasions; from formal, through casual, to adventure sports, with materials that complement alpaca such as wool, and other fibers; which gives a unique versatility to this collection and being 100% eco friendly
Most of these yarns has been developed with our Easy-Wash technology, which allows the fiber to be machine washed without damage, thanks to its innovative anti-shrinkage enzymatic treatment that is 100% environmentally friendly.

Color Trend & Natural Tones
The A/W 24-25 color chart surprises with its lively, dazzling palette and the perfect shades to spice up this season.
The colors of this latest collection have been developed following the upcoming trends proposed by the main fashion houses around the world. In the same way, we offer shades exactly adapted to the annual prediction of trend colors for the 24/25 season.,

Stock Service
Our Stock Service offers a series of yarns for those who are developing a new collection or starting a business around the alpaca.
This line explores a wide range of textures thanks to the great variety of yarns it presents. It stands out our classic yarns in pure alpaca and fancy yarns in alpaca and blends with other natural fibres. Its captivating colour palette reflects the character of the season, with its dark and earthy tones that evoke nature and the winter atmosphere.
