New Yarn & Colour Innovation

Michell Labs was born as the platform of our Research and Development area to present innovations in yarns and colours. We are constantly seeking, working and developing new qualities, new uses, properties and colours that aim to present our customers our latest innovations. At Michell, we pay close attention for innovating around the concept of sustainability, which will help us to work in a friendly way to care about our environment, our world.


Michell labs will present through each season new developments that will be informed by our sales area accordingly. Also as part of the Michell Labs, we put our R&D area at your service to give the possibility for developing your own unique, exclusive and personalised yarn, colour and/or any marketing service you may need, as we have a full commitment to be flexible and make your sourcing experience as easy as possible.
